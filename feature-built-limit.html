Built Limit Function

The first step is to have your credit card with a pre-approved limit.

Who is this role for?

The Build Limit Function with the ETbank card is for people we don't know well enough to offer a credit limit.

What is it for?

The objective is precisely to get to know you better and understand how you organize yourself to be able to limit yourself in the future.

How does the Build Limit Function work?

If you receive the Build Limit Function, your credit card will be a little different from what you know. It works like this:

Set aside an amount from your account to use as a limit

Make your purchases on credit using a physical or virtual card

Pay your credit card bill by the due date

More than half of the people who follow these steps earn a limit in the first month.
