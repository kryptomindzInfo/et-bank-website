What is Pix

Pix is ​​a payment method created by the Central Bank. With it, you can make and receive instant transfers, pay bills and make purchases at any time using your cell phone. It is a fast and free alternative to boleto, TED, DOC and even debit cards, using your account balance.

What are Pix keys

Instead of using all bank details to receive transfers or payments, passing a single piece of information, called a key, the money arrives in your account in seconds.

What can be registered as a Pix key

CPF or CNPJ

Phone number

E-mail

Random key

But it's worth remembering: Pix keys serve to facilitate the process, you can even use your bank details to make a Pix.
